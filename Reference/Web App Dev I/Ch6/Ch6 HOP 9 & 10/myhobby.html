Photography

I took up photography as a hobby in my senior year of high school after taking a photography class. I became very interested in taking pictures afterward. I was further encouraged to keep taking photos during my family's vacation in Hawaii.

This Christmas, I saved all the money I got in order to purchase a Nikon D7500 DSLR camera. I have been frequently taking pictures since then. I typically drive in a random direction until I find something interesting to take a picture of.
